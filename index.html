WELCOME TO GHANIAN CULTURE

ABOUT

Ghana is a country in the West of Africa where, newborns are given special names depending on the day they are born.The Akan people use the Kwa language group’s system of timekeeping, which is based on a six-day week (‘nnanson‘), with the seventh day inclusive.
